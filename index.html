Dr.Vivekanandan

Tamil actor-comedian and Padma Shri awardee

Here is a time line of Mr.Vivek's Life:

  • 1961 - Born in Sankarankovil, Tenkasi, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • (1978-81) - He holds B.Com Degree from The American College,Madurai.
  • 1987 - Made a debute in Tamil Cinema flim,Manathil Uruthi Vendum.
  • (2002-04) - Three consecutive Flim Fare Awards for Best Comedian in Tamil Industry
  • 2006 - Kalaivanar Award for his contributions to Tamil Cinema
  • 2007 - Gave a blockbuster movie "Sivaji:The Boss" as a Comedian.
  • 2008-11 - International Tamil Flim Award for Best Comedian
  • 2009 - Grabbed a Padma Shri for his contributions to Indian Cinema.
  • 2009 - Recieved Honour Special Jury Award
  • 2009 - Recieved Asianet Flim Award for Best Comedian
  • 2010 - Vivek launched Green Kalam,a project to plant trees across India inspired by former President of India A. P. J. Abdul Kalam.Kalam insisted the project should not be named after him, after which Vivek briefly changed its name to Green Globe.As of his death, over 3,300,000 saplings had been planted.
  • 2015 - Recived Honorary Doctorate from Sathyabama University.
  • 2021 - Vivek passed away on April 17, 2021 at a SIMS Hospital in Chennai, following a massive heart attack. He was 59
